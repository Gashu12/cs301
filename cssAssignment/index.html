Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

    Lemon Merimgue pie
  • 9-inch pie
  • 30 Min - Prep time
  • Min - Cook time
  • Min - Total
  • Servings

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbsp white sugar

    • DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
      • 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
      • in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
      • over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
      • in butter.
      • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
      • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
      • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
      • Remove from heat.
      • filling into baked pastry shell.
      • In a large glass or metal bowl ...
      • egg whites until foamy.
      • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
      • Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
    3. Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
